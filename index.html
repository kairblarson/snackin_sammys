ABOUT SNACKIN SAMMY'S


Snackin Sammy's: One Plate at a Time

I've struggled with trichotillomania for years, leaving me feeling insecure and different. It wasn't just about my lashes it was about how I felt about myself. Everything changed when I discovered mascara. It gave me the confidence I'd been missing, reminding me that beauty isn't just about appearance

The real turning point came when I met Nina, who shared my struggles. Her strength inspired me, and after her passing, I knew I wanted to honor her legacy. That's how Evaura Cosmetics was born.

Evaura isn't just mascara it's a symbol of self love. Our formula gives you fuller lashes, but the true magic is in the confidence it brings. We understand insecurity, and we want you to know you are beautiful, just as you are. Evaura helps you embrace your uniqueness and feel confident every day.

With Evaura, you're not just wearing mascara; you're wearing your confidence. You are beautiful, strong, and deserving of love and we're here to help you see it.

20% of all proceeds go directly to the TLC Foundation!
